Scaling Data Organizations

Case Study: Scaling Data Impact at Nickel, a 5M+ customer fintech

Organization Impact

0 → 40 Data Team Growth
800+ Data Consumers
200+ Warehouse Users

Established a hybrid organization model where embedded analysts gained full autonomy through centralized tools and platforms. This framework enabled teams to ingest, transform, and deploy data products independently while maintaining governance standards.

Grew the data organization through strategic hiring, clear career paths, and a balance between central platform teams and embedded business analysts. Maintained high standards and governance while scaling rapidly.

Democratized data access from 20 to 800+ business users consuming data insights, through self-service platforms, training programs, and simple-but-powerful tools that prioritized autonomy over complexity.

Enabled teams to launch real-time personalized notifications, fraud scoring, AML alerting, regulatory reporting, and many more data-driven products across the organization using standardized platforms.

Below are examples of high-impact data apps I've built to deliver tangible business value.

Advanced Visualizations for Operational Teams

Advanced visualizations beyond typical BI tools—like network graph views—optimized for operational teams.

Customer Data Platform

Mobile App Personalization
